# Privacy Policy — AWAtv

**Last updated:** 2026-04-27

This Privacy Policy describes how AWAtv ("we", "us", "the app") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile, TV, desktop, or web applications (collectively, the "Service").

## 1. Information you provide

### Playlist credentials
When you add an M3U URL or Xtream Codes account, AWAtv stores those credentials **locally on your device** in the platform's secure storage (iOS Keychain, Android Keystore via flutter_secure_storage). They are **never transmitted to our servers**.

### Account information (optional, premium only)
If you create an AWAtv account to enable cross-device sync:

- Email address (for sign-in via magic link)
- Display name (optional)
- Locale (auto-detected, user-overridable)

### Payment information
Subscription payments are processed by **Apple App Store, Google Play Billing, or RevenueCat**. We never see or store your payment card information.

## 2. Information generated by your use of the Service

### Stored on your device only
- Watched programmes / playback positions (history)
- Favourites
- Preferences (theme, language, parental control PIN — hashed)
- EPG cache
- TMDB metadata cache

### Synced to our servers (premium only, opt-in)
If you enable cloud sync, the following is synced to encrypt-at-rest Supabase storage:

- Favourites (item ids only — no playlist URLs)
- Watch history (item ids + position seconds — no playlist URLs)
- Device sessions (anonymous device id, kind: phone/tablet/tv/desktop, last seen)
- Profile fields (display name, locale)

Playlist URLs and Xtream credentials are **never synced** to our servers — they remain device-local.

### Telemetry (opt-in only)
If you opt in via Settings → Privacy, anonymised crash reports and usage analytics are sent to:
- **Sentry** (crash reports)
- **PostHog** (anonymised events: feature usage, performance)

You can opt out at any time. We do not use third-party advertising trackers when telemetry is off.

## 3. Information NOT collected

- We do not track your IP address beyond standard server logs (90-day retention).
- We do not share data with advertising networks.
- We do not sell user data.
- We do not store your playlist content or credentials on our servers.

## 4. Advertising (free tier)

The free tier shows banner and interstitial ads via **Google AdMob**. AdMob may collect a personalised advertising id (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android). You can:

- Reset your advertising id in your device settings
- Limit ad tracking in your device settings
- Upgrade to **Premium** to remove all ads

## 5. Third-party services

| Service | Purpose | Data shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| TMDB (themoviedb.org) | Metadata enrichment | Movie/series titles you look up |
| Supabase | Account + sync (premium) | Account email, sync data |
| RevenueCat | Subscription management | Apple/Google receipt validation |
| Apple, Google | Payments | Per their own privacy policies |
| Google AdMob | Ads (free tier) | Advertising id |
| Sentry | Crash reporting (opt-in) | Anonymised crash dumps |
| PostHog | Analytics (opt-in) | Anonymised event names |

Each service has its own privacy policy.

## 6. Your rights (GDPR, CCPA, KVKK)

You have the right to:

- **Access** your data — request a JSON export at `support@awatv.app`
- **Correct** your data — via Settings → Account
- **Delete** your data — via Settings → Account → Delete account, or email `support@awatv.app`
- **Export** your data — via Settings → Account → Export
- **Opt out of analytics** — via Settings → Privacy
- **Withdraw consent** — disable sync any time

For Turkey users (KVKK): you can exercise the rights above and additionally request information about with whom your data has been shared.

## 7. Data retention

- Account data: retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
- Sync data: same as account data.
- Telemetry: anonymised, retained 90 days.
- Server logs: 90 days.

## 8. Children's privacy

AWAtv is rated **17+** because it is a generic media player and we cannot vet what content the user's playlist points to. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Parental control PIN-gating is available in Settings.

## 9. Changes to this policy

We will post any changes here and notify you in-app at least 14 days before changes take effect.
