AWAtv is the premium player you bring your own playlist to.

Add any M3U / M3U8 file or Xtream Codes account once and AWAtv organises your live channels, movies, and series into a beautifully arranged library — with rich metadata, posters, ratings, plot summaries, EPG, and trailers powered by TMDB.

KEY FEATURES

• Universal playlist support — M3U, M3U8, and the full Xtream Codes API
• Smart EPG — XMLTV ingestion with gzip support, now-and-next strip, programme details
• Rich metadata — automatic enrichment from TMDB (posters, backdrops, plot, cast, ratings, trailers)
• Smart episode tracking — resume where you left off, watched markers, next-up suggestions
• Smooth playback — libmpv-powered engine handles HEVC, AV1, HLS, DASH; large adaptive buffer for slow connections
• Beautiful design — dark-first interface with glassmorphism accents, hero animations, and Material 3 motion
• Cross-platform — same account on iPhone, iPad, Android phone, Android TV, Apple TV, macOS, Windows
• Cloud sync (Premium) — favourites, history, and parental controls across all your devices
• Multi-screen (Premium) — watch on multiple devices simultaneously
• No ads (Premium) — clean, distraction-free viewing

ABOUT YOUR DATA

AWAtv does not host or distribute any content. You provide your own playlist URL or Xtream account. Credentials are stored locally in your device's secure keychain — never on our servers (premium cloud sync syncs only metadata: favourites, watched markers, preferences).
